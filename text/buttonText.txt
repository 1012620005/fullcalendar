
buttonText
==========

Text that will be displayed on buttons of the header.

<div class='spec' markdown='1'>
Object, *default*:

	{
		prev:     'prev',
		next:     'next',
		prevYear: 'prev year',
		nextYear: 'next year',
		today:    'today',
		month:    'month',
		week:     'week',
		day:      'day'
	}
</div>

The [buttonIcons](../display/buttonIcons) and [themeButtonIcons](../display/themeButtonIcons) options take precedence over `buttonText`.

HTML injection is not supported. All special characters will be escaped.
