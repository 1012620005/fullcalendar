
dayPopoverFormat *2.1.0*
========================

Determines the date format of title of the popover created by the [eventLimitClick](../display/eventLimitClick) option.

<div class='spec' markdown='1'>
String
</div>

Must be a [date formatting string](../utilities/date_formatting_string).
The default value is `"dddd, MMMM D"` for English and `"LL"` for other languages.
