
axisFormat
==========

<div class='removed-notice' markdown='1'>
This method has been removed in v3. Please use [slotLabelFormat]() instead.
</div>

Determines the time-text that will be displayed on the vertical axis of the agenda views.

<div class='spec' markdown='1'>
[format string](../utilities/date_formatting_string), *default*: `'h(:mm)a'`
</div>

The default value depends on the current [lang](../text/lang).

The default English value will produce times that look like "5pm" and "5:30pm".
