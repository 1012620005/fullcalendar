
listDayFormat *3.0*
===================

<div class='spec' markdown='1'>
String, `false`
</div>

A [date formatting string](../utilities/date_formatting_string/)
that affects the text on the left side of the day headings in list view.

<img src='../../../pages/docs/_images/listDayFormat.png' width='468' height='259' style='box-shadow: 0 1px 4px rgba(0,0,0,.3)' />

If `false` is specified, no text is displayed.
