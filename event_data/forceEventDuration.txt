
forceEventDuration
==================

A flag to force calculation of an event's `end` if it is unspecified.

<div class='spec' markdown='1'>
Boolean, *default:* `false`
</div>

If an event's `end` is not specified, it will be calculated and assigned to
the [Event Object]() using
[defaultTimedEventDuration]() or [defaultAllDayEventDuration]().
